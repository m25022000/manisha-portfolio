My Innovation Perspective

AI transformation should not begin with the question, “What can we automate?” It should begin with, “What problem are we solving, who will be affected, and how will we know the change is creating responsible value?”

My approach combines technical knowledge with people-centered leadership. I would use readiness assessments, small pilot programs, transparent communication, measurable adoption goals, and continuous feedback before scaling an AI solution across an organization.
